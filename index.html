A convergence of multiple ideas over the centuries ...
  • Buddhism—second arrow, anicca (impermanence), sati (mindfulness)
  • Stoicism—Epictetus, Marcus Aurelius; amor fati, acceptance of what is
  • Taoism—wu wei, water wearing away rock
  • Vedanta—sakshi (witness consciousness)
  • Zen—shikantaza, objectless awareness
  • Sufism—Rumi: “The wound is where the Light enters you”
  • Eckhart / Christianity—the eternal now, Gelassenheit (releasement)
  • Islam—surrender, tawakkul (trust in divine unfolding)
  • Ubuntu—“I am because we are”
  • Bhagavad Gita—karma yoga, lila (divine play)
  • Process philosophy—Whitehead’s occasions of experience
  • Physics—quantum complementarity, Prigogine’s dissipative structures
  • Neuroscience—default mode network, narrative self as construct
  • CBT—Beck, Neff; cognitive distortions, self-compassion

Breath-Reactive Mode
